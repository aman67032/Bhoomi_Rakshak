Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters related to active illegal mining operations, please use our emergency hotline for immediate assistance.

How can I volunteer with Bhoomi Rakshak?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including community outreach, environmental monitoring, legal research, and technical support. Please select "Volunteer Opportunities" in the contact form, and our volunteer coordinator will get in touch with details about our current needs and how your skills can best contribute.

Do you provide legal assistance for communities affected by illegal mining?

Yes, we work with a network of environmental lawyers who provide pro bono or reduced-cost legal services to affected communities. Contact us with details about your situation, and we'll connect you with appropriate legal resources.

Can I donate to support your work?

Absolutely! As a non-profit organization, we rely on donations to sustain our environmental protection efforts. You can make a one-time or recurring donation through our secure payment gateway. All donations are tax-deductible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Please select "Donations & Support" in the contact form for more information.
