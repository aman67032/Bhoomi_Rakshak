Supreme Court Orders Stricter Enforcement of Mining Regulations in Protected Areas

In a landmark judgment, the Supreme Court of India has directed all state governments to implement stricter monitoring and enforcement of mining regulations in and around protected forest areas and wildlife sanctuaries.

The court has mandated the formation of special task forces in each state to conduct regular inspections and take immediate action against illegal mining operations within a 10km radius of ecologically sensitive zones.
